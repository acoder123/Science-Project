Suggestions for your daily diet and health:

    The benefits of Calcium:

  • Calcium can prevent several diseases, such as cancer.
  • Calcium strengthens your bones and keeps your bodily functions working well.
  • Calcium can prevent blood clotting in your veins.
  • *Note: The recommended daily intake of Calcium per day is 1,000 mg for adults only while children need 1,300 mg of Calcium.

Why fat in your daily diet should be reduced to better your wellbeing:

  • Fat can cause cardiovascular disease which can lead to heart attacks and death.
  • Fat can cause high blood pressure.
  • Obesity (caused by excess intake of fat and sugar), has been linked to many types of cancer

Important Notice!

While fat is unhealthy in excess amounts, the USDA recommends an average adult to eat 44g to 65g of fat on a daily basis in a 2,000 calorie diet. Fat is essential for Energy storage, Vitamin absorption, Forming cell membranes, Insulating the body and protecting organs.

A few healthy foods that can make a big difference in your diet:

  • Fat free Milk is a great source of Calcium. It has 300mg of Calcium and a measly 0.2g of fat.
  • Cottage Cheese also provides a great source of Calcium with low fat. It contains 138g of Calcium and only 0.7g of fat.
  • Do not forget that fat is also important. Mozzarella cheese contains 207g of Calcium, however, it has a healthy 6.3g of fat.
  • Plain yogurt(low fat) contains 430g of calcium and 3.8g of fat


Summary

  1. Calcium is important to your daily diet. It is recommended to have 1,000 mg of Calcium per day for adults, and 1,300mg of calcium for children.
  2. While fat is unhealthy in excessive amounts, it is still an important part of your diet.
  3. Not all foods have the same amount of nutritious value as others, which is why you should take care of your diet.
